FlatCon 2015

FlatCon 2015 is back and it has much in store from October 23-25, 2015. There will be a tournament featuring the leagues of: D&D Adventurer's League, Warhammer, and Pathfinder Society. All of this will be happening at the Interstate Center Mini Expo Building.

Alright arcade fans! This convention is where it is going to be for all the classic retro gaming style adventures!
